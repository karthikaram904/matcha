FAQ
Subscription questions
What's included in each box?

Your chosen tier's matcha tins, a handwritten harvest note card, and any surprise equipment or samples for Ceremony and Sensei tiers.

Can I pause or cancel?

Yes — pause for up to 3 months or cancel anytime from your account dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-ins of any kind.

When does my box ship each month?

Boxes are dispatched on the 1st of each month. You will receive a tracking link by email as soon as your box is collected. Delivery takes 2–5 days depending on your location.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely — change your tier or billing cycle any time before the 28th of the month. The new plan takes effect from the following month's dispatch.

What if I don't like my tin?

We stand behind every tin we send. If you are unsatisfied for any reason, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we will send a replacement or issue a full refund — no questions asked.
