We believe matcha is more than a drink — it is a living philosophy. Meet the team devoted to bringing that philosophy to India.

Who We Are
Rooted in tradition

Chado Matcha was founded in 2019 by a group of tea enthusiasts who fell in love with Japan's tea culture during a pilgrimage through Kyoto's Uji district. We came back determined to source the finest ceremonial matcha directly from the farmers who grow it — and share it with India.

Today we partner with three family farms in Uji, each with generations of expertise in shade-growing and stone-grinding. Every tin travels from farm to your door in under 72 hours — sealed at peak freshness.

Our Foundation
Mission, Vision & Values

Three pillars that guide every decision — from the farms we partner with to the workshops we run.

Our Mission
Bring authentic

Four principles borrowed from the Way of Tea itself — harmony, respect, purity, and tranquility — guide every sourcing decision, customer interaction, and cup we brew.

What We Stand For
Four principles, one practice
01
Direct Sourcing

We visit every partner farm personally. No brokers, no blends — just a farmer's name and harvest date on every tin.

02
Freshness First

Matcha oxidises fast. Every tin is air-sealed within 72 hours of grinding and dispatched without touching a warehouse shelf.

03
Craft Over Convenience

Traditional granite millstones cost more and grind slower. We choose them anyway — because the flavour difference is everything.

04
Ceremony

We teach the ritual alongside selling the ingredient. Workshops, harvest notes, and a growing community of practitioners.
