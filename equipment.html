The Matcha Journey
From farm to your cup

Four steps. Centuries of craft. Zero shortcuts.

01
Cultivation

Shade cloth is drawn over the plants 21 days before harvest, boosting chlorophyll and L-theanine while suppressing.

02
Harvest

Only the finest first-flush leaves are selected by hand. The stems and veins are removed, leaving pure tencha — the raw form.

03
Stone Grinding

Tencha is fed slowly into traditional granite millstones. One stone grinds just 30g per hour — preserving every amino.

04
Packaging

Tins are nitrogen-flushed and sealed within 72 hours of grinding. No warehouse, no ageing — straight to your door at peak freshness.
